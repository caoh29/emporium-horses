About the Company

Welcome to Emporium Horses, where passion meets excellence in the world of equestrianism. Founded with a vision to provide top-notch horses and services, we have been dedicated to fostering a deep connection between riders and their equine companions. Our commitment to quality, integrity, and the well-being of both horses and riders sets us apart in the industry.

Company History

Emporium Horses was founded in 1920 by Charles Horton. Since our inception, we have grown to become a leading name in the equestrian world, offering a diverse range of horse breeds, exceptional training programs, and unparalleled customer service. Our journey is marked by a passion for horses and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Certifications

We take pride in our certifications from renowned organizations in the equestrian industry. These certifications reflect our commitment to maintaining the highest standards in horse breeding, care, and training. Our dedication to excellence is certified by Horsemanship Association, American Horse Council and The Board for Equine Interaction Professionals.

Clients

Emporium Horses is honored to have served a diverse range of clients, including passionate riders, equestrian enthusiasts, and professionals. Our horses have become trusted companions for riders of all levels, and we cherish the relationships we've built with our valued clients.

Awards

Our commitment to excellence has been recognized through various awards in the equestrian community. We are proud to have received accolades for our outstanding contributions to horse breeding, training, and overall industry excellence.

Training and Conditioning Center

At Emporium Horses, we boast a state-of-the-art training and conditioning center designed to nurture the physical and mental well-being of our horses. Our experienced trainers are dedicated to providing comprehensive training programs tailored to each horse's unique needs, ensuring they reach their full potential.
